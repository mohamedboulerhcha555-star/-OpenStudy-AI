## 📖 Overview

OpenStudy AI is an open-source educational platform designed to help students learn faster and more efficiently using artificial intelligence.

Instead of spending hours creating revision materials, students can upload their notes, PDFs, or textbooks and instantly receive AI-generated summaries, flashcards, quizzes, explanations, and personalized study plans.

Our mission is to make high-quality AI-assisted education accessible to everyone.
